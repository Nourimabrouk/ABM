## Exemplar Papers (Models for Your Research)

### SAOM Applications in School Networks

1. **Shani et al. (2023)** - "Together for Tolerance" Feasibility Study
   - Paper: https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.3233/DEV-230342
   - Data & Code: https://osf.io/rk2sd/
   *Network intervention using social referents to spread equality-based respect norms in German schools*

2. **Zingora et al. (2025)** - "Spurious Effects in School Networks"
   - https://bpspsychub.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/bjso.12797
   *Critical meta-analysis showing importance of controlling for social influence in SAOM*

3. **Long (2024)** - "Ingroup Norms of Multiculturalism vs Tolerance"
   - https://bpspsychub.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1111/bjso.12778
   *Distinguishes between tolerance and multiculturalism effects on intergroup solidarity*

### Complex Contagion & Network Dynamics

4. **Aiyappa et al. (2024)** - "Emergence of Simple and Complex Contagion"
   - https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/sciadv.adh4439
   - PDF: https://www.science.org/doi/pdf/10.1126/sciadv.adh4439
   *Foundational paper on how resistance mechanisms generate complex contagion from cognitive processes*

5. **Contreras et al. (2024)** - "Infection Patterns in Contagion Processes"
   - https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371/journal.pcbi.1012206
   *Distinguishing simple vs complex contagion for intervention targeting*

6. **PNAS (2024)** - "Social Learning with Complex Contagion"
   - https://www.pnas.org/doi/10.1073/pnas.2414291121
   *How multiple exposures affect behavior adoption beyond material payoffs*

## Quality Benchmarks for Your Research

### Model Convergence Standards
- t-ratios < 0.1 for all parameters
- Overall maximum convergence ratio < 0.25
- Minimum 2000 iterations in Phase 3

### Goodness-of-Fit Requirements
- Indegree, outdegree, and triad census distributions
- Behavioral distribution matching
- Geodesic distance distribution tests

### Publication Standards
- Preregistration of analysis plan
- Open data and code availability
- Detailed supplementary materials with robustness checks
