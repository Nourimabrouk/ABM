## Core Methodological Framework

### Research Design Principles
```
Study Type: Computational Social Science / Agent-Based Modeling
Design: Quasi-experimental simulation with empirically calibrated parameters
Population: German high school students (ethnic majority and minority)
Setting: Classroom social networks in educational institutions
Intervention: Hypothetical tolerance intervention with systematic parameter variation
```

### Validity Framework
1. **Internal Validity**: Causal inference from intervention to outcomes
2. **External Validity**: Generalizability beyond German high school context
3. **Construct Validity**: Theoretical constructs accurately measured and modeled
4. **Statistical Conclusion Validity**: Appropriate statistical methods and inference
